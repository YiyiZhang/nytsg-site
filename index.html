About Us

New York Tech for Social Good is an organization for anyone in NYC interested in technology AND social good! We primarily operate via OUR MEETUP PAGE.

You do not need to have a particular skill set or a skill level to join this group. You don't need a background in technology either. All you need to have is an affinity for social impact. This group is right for you if:

  • You have technology skills but you're not quite sure how to utilize them to give back to society
  • You have great ideas but you don't necessarily have a background in technology
  • You simply want to meet some good people and learn more about tech for good
